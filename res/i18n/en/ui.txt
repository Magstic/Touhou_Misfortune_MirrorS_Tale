What are Spell Cards?
Special bullet patterns fired by
boss enemies. Used when hit by
your bomb/damage.
*Self-attack corresponds to bomb
Spell Card Bonus
A bonus earned by defeating the
opponent's spell without missing
or using bombs.
Spell history can be viewed in
the Replay menu's Result.
◇Extend
When point items collected reach 50,
125, 200, 300, 450, 600, and every
200 after, you gain 1 extra life.
◇Death Bombing
If you activate a bomb (spell card)
at the moment you get hit by enemy
fire, you can revive without dying.
◇Auto Item Collection
Moving top of screen at max power
Using your spell card (bomb)
Boss pattern shifts or is defeated
◇Save Method
Press # key on the Result screen
after clearing a stage to save the
replay of that stage.
※Cannot save if you continued
Saved replays can be played back from
Replay in the title continue menu.
◇Special Controls
Left key: (while held) Slow playback
Right key: (while held) Fast playback
◇Download from Homepage
From 東方氷幻鏡>ﾘﾌﾟﾚｲﾀﾞｳﾝﾛｰﾄﾞ
on website, you can download replays.
※Please to the website for details
◇Upload to Homepage
If you have registered as a user on
the site, you can upload your replay
data to the web.
※Uploaded replay data may be
downloaded by other people.
◇Saving to SD Card
･Replay data saved in the app can be
saved to an SD card, up to a maximum
of 10 replays.
※SD card usage can be enabled/
disabled in the options settings.
◇How to Join Rankings?
You need to register as a user on the
site. For details, please see the user
registration page on the site.
After registering, press right soft
key to upload scores.
◇Registration Notes
Please avoid entering text in your
username or comments that others may
find offensive.
◇Differences and Strategy Hints
･Extra allows item collection at top.

･Always starts with 3 players.

･Continue unavailable.
･Enemy bullets follow fixed patterns
based on your movement. Memorize
dodging patterns to clear easily.
